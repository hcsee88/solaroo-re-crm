2. apps/api/src/common/authz/authz.service.ts
   ADDED — getBestScope(user, resource, action): Promise<Scope | null>
   Returns highest scope among all user permissions for a resource:action.
   Precedence: all(4) > team(3) > assigned(2) > own(1) > null.
   Used in service list methods to determine WHERE clause scope filter.
