ROLE:     DESIGN_LEAD
PURPOSE:  Head of Design Department — technical oversight across sales-to-delivery
          lifecycle. Bridges the handover from pre-sales technical work to project
          execution. Approves all design artefacts.

POSITIONING:
  DESIGN_LEAD sits above DESIGN_ENGINEER in technical authority.
  They are NOT a sales role and should not create or edit commercial records.
  They are NOT a project manager — they do not control project timelines, budgets,
  or gate approvals except for design-specific gates when directly assigned.
  They DO own all things technical: assessments, drawings, documents, design quality.

V1 DECISION RATIONALE:
  The practical problem this role solves is:
    "Who can actually approve a drawing or a technical assessment before it goes
     to the Director for final sign-off?"
  In V1, DIRECTOR is the only approver for drawings and technical assessments.
  This creates a bottleneck. DESIGN_LEAD relieves it at the design level.

  The Design Lead also needs full pipeline and project visibility to plan design
  resource — they cannot plan without knowing what is coming in the funnel.

  They explicitly should NOT access finance/commercial records, user management,
  or have the power to create/approve opportunities or proposals.
